Who We Are

Your Reliable Travel Partner

Travel With Choudhary is one of the trusted taxi service providers based in Katra, Jammu & Kashmir. We specialize in comfortable, affordable and safe taxi services for pilgrims and tourists visiting Jammu, Vaishno Devi, Kashmir, Amritsar, Himachal Pradesh and Leh Ladakh.

Whether you need a local taxi, airport transfer, railway pickup, sightseeing trip or a complete holiday package, our experienced drivers ensure a smooth and memorable journey.

We believe every journey should be safe, comfortable and affordable. Our clean vehicles, professional drivers and transparent pricing have made us the preferred travel partner for hundreds of happy customers.
